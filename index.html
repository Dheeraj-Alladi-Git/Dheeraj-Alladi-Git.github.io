About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Dheeraj Alladi. a Java Full Stack Developer with 6+ years of experience building scalable, secure, and cloud-native enterprise applications across banking, healthcare, and e-commerce domains.

I specialize in designing and developing backend microservices using Java, Spring Boot, and RESTful APIs, with a strong focus on system design, security, and performance. I have hands-on experience working with event-driven architectures, authentication mechanisms like OAuth 2.0 and JWT, and relational databases such as PostgreSQL, MySQL, and Oracle.

On the frontend, I have built responsive and data-driven user interfaces using React, Angular, and modern JavaScript, collaborating closely with product, QA, and DevOps teams to deliver end-to-end features.

I have worked extensively on cloud platforms such as AWS and Azure, deploying containerized applications using Docker and Kubernetes, and automating CI/CD pipelines with Jenkins and Git-based workflows. My goal is to build reliable, maintainable systems that solve real-world business problems at scale.
